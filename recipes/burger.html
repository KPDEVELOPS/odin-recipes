Burger Recipe

Description

This recipe was created by my great, great, great grandfather. He was an absolute genius when it came to creating the best burgers in his entire country. What you'll find here is a very special recipe that, until now, has been passed down only within the family.

Now, you get to experience the same burst of incredible flavors and happiness that my entire family has experienced because of the meticulous process of perfecting this burger that my great, great, great grandfather happily went through.

Ingredients

  • 95% Lean Beef
  • Kevin's Secret Sauce [Coming Soon]
  • Brioche Buns
  • Pepper Jack Cheese
  • Red Onion
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Dice Onions
  2. Roast Onions
  3. Cook Beef Patties
  4. Toast Brioche Buns
  5. Melt Cheese on Patty
  6. Add Kevin's Secret Sauce To Toasted Buns
  7. Stack in the following order; Bottom Bun, Patty with Cheese, Onion, Top Bun
